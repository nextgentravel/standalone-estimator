Welcome kit for new departmental travel leads

Welcome. We have compiled a welcome package to help you succeed in your role as a Senior Full-time Travel Officer (SFTO) or a Departmental Travel Card Coordinator (DTCC).

Each document includes information on your respective roles and responsibilities, travel policies, guidelines and directives, and Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution.

New SFTOs, SFTO delegate, DTCCs, DTCC delegates or regional DTCCs must submit a Department or agency set-up form if you are a within their organization.

Note: When completing the form, you must check a box in each fillable section before the remaining fields can be edited.
