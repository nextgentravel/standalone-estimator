Shared Travel Services

STS manages GC’s business travel solution provided by AmexGBT, previously named the Hogg Robinson Group (HRG). This travel solution provides the following services:

  • online travel management system providing travel information and tools such as
    • online booking tool for researching travel and making reservations
    • expense management tool for submitting travel and expense requests
    • travel reporting for departments and agencies to better manage travel spending
    • learning management system that hosts training resources
  • full-service travel agency and call-center staffed by knowledgeable counsellors
  • government travel cards

STS also works with departmental travel coordinators for each department who support their travel requirements, guidelines and reporting through:

  • Senior Full-Time Travel Officers (SFTO)
  • Departmental Travel Card Coordinators (DTCC)

If you’re a DTCC, check out the latest news at Hogg Robinson Group’s Shared Travel Services site.

NextGEN Travel

The Next Generation (NextGEN) Travel Program is a team within Public Services and Procurement Canada with a mandate to re-imagine the future of travel for the GC as the current GC Travel system will expire in a few years. We are focusing on a solution that works for all users, so they can focus on their work and what really matters. Learn more about NextGEN Travel and how we’re co-creating the next travel solution with users.

Our user-centric approach

To better understand GC’s business travel experiences, the NextGEN Travel team adopted a design-thinking approach to reframe how we design the future travel solution. By placing the traveller first, we hope to reframe travel so that it is simpler, accessible, innovative and intuitive .

In 2018 to 2019, NextGEN Travel held open houses and innovation labs with public servants to brainstorm the future of GC travel. We visited 8 cities and spoke with more than 750 public servants from many departments and across all levels.

See what we heard from users about their travel experiences.

Product development

We’ve taken NextGEN Travel’s user research conducted and heard what users struggle with and what they hope to see in a future travel solution. Key areas have been identified that we can address now. To support users, we’ve created a Government of Canada Travel Calculator that is independent of logging into the AmexGBT travel management solution. This calculator can help support annual travel planning, updating your travel plan information or to get a quick travel estimate for ad-hoc or last minute travel.
