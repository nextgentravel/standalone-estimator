Book travel

All Government of Canada (GC) business travel is booked through the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution. In this page, you will find information on how to book travel, accommodations, and other services through the AmexGBT travel management solution.

Create a travel request

Create a travel request within the AmexGBT travel management solution to start the booking process. This step is important, no matter how you travel or where you stay.

AmexGBT Travel management solution

Step 1: Book transportation

Select a method of transportation to get help on how to proceed in the AmexGBT travel management solution. When booking travel, refer to trip estimates you previously created in the Government of Canada Travel Calculator. Add justifications if your costs exceed your pre-approved estimate.

Flight

To book your flight:

  1. log in to the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution
  2. In the online booking tool, select Flight
  3. search for flight options by availability
  4. choose your desired departure and return flights
  5. proceed with your chosen flight selection
  6. review fare rules associated with your selected flights
  7. accept the terms and conditions
  8. add your selected flights to your itinerary
  9. complete the trip details only if you have completed all necessary bookings

Tip: When booking travel, consider using future travel credits (FTC) from your department. Future travel credits are unused funds from cancelled or rescheduled flights that are available in the AmexGBT travel management solution. Refer to the Future Travel Credits presentation and Applying future travel credits quick reference for further details.

Train

To book your train:

  1. log into the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution
  2. in the Online Booking Tool, select Rail
  3. search for train options
  4. choose your preferred outbound and inbound journeys
  5. review the total cost of carriage
  6. accept the terms and conditions of carriage
  7. click the “continue” button
  8. Review the inbound and outbound restrictions
  9. add your selected train to your itinerary
  10. complete the trip details only if you have completed all necessary bookings

Tip: When booking travel by train, refer to the list of Via Rail Station Codes (PDF, 391 KB).

Car rental

To book a car rental:

  1. log into the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution
  2. in the Online Booking Tool, select Car Rental
  3. search for car rental options and preferences which comply with the 2021 Accommodation and Car Rental Directory
  4. choose the desired vehicle type
  5. select the desired rate option
  6. review the rental details.
    Note: You may add a membership number for rental car company to your traveller profile if applicable
  7. accept the terms and conditions
  8. add the car rental to your itinerary
  9. complete the trip details only if you have completed all necessary bookings

For further assistance, consult section 6. Book Car Rental within the Creating a travel request guidebook.

For car rental insurance, you can use a subsidized federal rate by referring to the Car Rental Insurance (PDF, 1.4 MB) document.

Tip: For appropriate rates and approved car rental companies in Canada that have your desired vehicle type, please review the Search for car page. The Accommodation and Car Rental Directory (ACRD) also lists pre-negotiated rates to pay at your destination.

Private vehicle

You can use your personal vehicle when travelling shorter distances. However, you must submit your travel request via the AmexGBT travel management solution. To determine the payable kilometric rates for travelling using your own vehicle, consult the National Travel Directive.

Step 2: Book hotel or accommodations

Hotel

To book a hotel:

  1. log into the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution
  2. in the Online Booking Tool, select Hotel
  3. search for hotel options
  4. choose a hotel and room
  5. review the hotel details
    Note: You may add a membership number to your traveller profile if applicable
  6. review the cancellation rules
  7. add the hotel to your itinerary
  8. complete the trip details only if you have completed all necessary bookings

For further assistance, you can consult section 5. Book Hotel within the Creating a travel request guidebook.

Tip: You should book hotels that offer GC negotiated rates. Please refer to the list of preferred hotels.

Private accommodations

You may choose to use private accommodations instead of a hotel. However, you must include this in the Expense Management Tool (EMT) as an out-of-pocket expense. This applies only to departments and agencies that use the AmexGBT EMT. If your department uses an independent expensing tool, contact your Senior Full-Time Travel Officer (SFTO) or your department’s finance team for instructions.

Private accommodations are still subject to National Travel Directive.

Meals and incidentals

While you travel on behalf of GC for business, you are eligible for compensation for your meals and incidentals. For the rates applicable for your trip, consult the National Travel Directive for Canadian cities for allowances or for International rates for allowances.

For assistance in adding meals, incidentals and private accommodations expenses, consult section 12. Add Meals, Incidentals and Private Accommodations within the Creating a travel request guidebook.

Tip: If you don’t pay for some meals during your travel, do not include them as an expense. For example: meals included in the airfare, as a part of your accommodation, or provided at an event should not be included.

Step 3: Payment methods

To complete accounting and payment details:

  1. review the payment information (either your personal credit or an IDTC card)
  2. complete your departmental accounting details page
  3. review travel request details
  4. create a trip name, which can be helpful if travelling often
  5. create the travel request

For further assistance, you can consult section 9. Accounting and payment details in the Creating a travel request guidebook.

Tip: Air and rail transportation are centrally billed. Therefore the Departmental Travel Expense Account should appear as the payment method. Travellers should use their IDTC where possible for all authorized travel expenses, including accommodations, vehicle rentals, taxis, meals and other incidental expenses. Otherwise, they can use their personal credit card.

Step 4: Additional estimates

This step only applies to departments and agencies that use the AmexGBT EMT. If your department uses an independent expensing tool, contact your SFTO for instructions.

Before your trip is finalized, add all other additional estimates to your travel request for an accurate cost estimate . To add additional estimates:

  1. access the EMT
  2. choose the appropriate expense item
  3. enter the amount and any other necessary details to make it clear for the approver
  4. save all expenses once you are finished

For further assistance, you can consult section 10. Access the Travel Request in the Expense Management Tool and 11. Add Additional Estimates within the Creating a travel request guidebook.

Step 5: Submit for approval

Before you submit your travel request, notify your supervisor or approver. Success rates for bookings increase when requests are approved immediately. Delayed approvals may result in restarting the booking process as rates may fluctuate and availability may change.

For further assistance, you can consult section 17. Submit for Approval within the Creating a travel request guidebook.
