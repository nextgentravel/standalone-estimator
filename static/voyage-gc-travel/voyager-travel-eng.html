Travel

Looking for help while travelling? This page contains tips and contact information to support you during travel.

Things to remember while travelling

During travel, remember to:

  • keep copies of your receipts
  • keep your documents safe and with you at all times
  • contact Amex Global Business Travel (GBT) travel specialists via the information on this page in an emergency

Travel support

AmexGBT Travel Specialists can be reached at any time.

  • 1-866-857-3578 (toll-free from Canada and the United States)
  • 1-613-822-3873 (collect call from other countries)

For those with hearing or speech impairments and who use a teletypewriter (TTY), an agent will assist you with all of the AmexGBT travel services:

  • 1-855-462-8870 (toll-free from Canada and the United States)
  • 1-704-417-5877 (collect call from other countries)
  • Email: usersupport.gc@amexgbt.com
