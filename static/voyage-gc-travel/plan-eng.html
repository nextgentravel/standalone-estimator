Plan travel

Planning a trip estimate for approval and key information to help you is easy.

Estimate travel

Whether you’re estimating your annual travel plan or improvising a travel estimate to discuss with your manager, this tool can help simplify your Government of Canada (GC) travel planning experience. This tool lets you create an estimate without logging into the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution.

This tool, however, does not replace the formal travel approval and booking process in the Shared Travel Services travel portal.

GC Travel Calculator

Create a preliminary travel estimate

When planning a trip, you'll need to estimate your travel costs. Use our GC Travel Calculator to create a quick estimate and send it to your supervisor for approval.

Needed items

Before booking your trip, have the following items ready:

  1. account and login information for the AmexGBT travel management solution
  2. valid personal identification (such as a passport, driver’s license, or entry visa)
  3. Individual Designated Travel Card (ITDC)
  4. provincial health card
  5. Public Service Health Care Plan card

Apply for an Individual Designated Travel Card, commonly referred to as a Government Travel Card, which offers a variety of services including insurance.

Tip: Plan for 6 weeks when you apply for the IDTC card to receive it in time to book your travel.
