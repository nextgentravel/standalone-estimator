Travel is returning

In the second year of the pandemic, provinces are reducing their travel restrictions, and we are seeing a return to travel. For Government of Canada (GC) employees required to travel for work, we have compiled helpful resources to stay informed of travel directives as the anticipated return to travel begins.

COVID-19 travel information

Travel directives and restrictions change quickly. We advise you to monitor updates on COVID-19 as your travel dates approach. Review guidelines for your city of departure, your destination, and locations you travel through. For reliable and up-to-date travel-related information on COVID-19, check out the following government sources.

  • Public Health Agency of Canada
  • Travel.gc.ca
