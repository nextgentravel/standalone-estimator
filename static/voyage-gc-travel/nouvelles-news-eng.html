Latest Amex Global Business Travel management solution updates

Keep up-to-date with recent and upcoming changes to the Amex Global Business Travel (AmexGBT) travel management solution.

Automated telephone system menu update

AmexGBT has updated their telephone system menu to enhance your user experience. When calling technical support, users can access menu options faster as well as quickly connect with an agent. All technical support telephone numbers are the same. Learn more about the updated telephone system menu.

Hogg Robinson Group email domain change

Hogg Robinson Group (HRG) is now a part of American Express Global Business Travel (GBT). HRG has been transitioning to the AmexGBT name and logo throughout their tools and processes for all its clients, including the Government of Canada. Any emails sent to the HRG domain trigger an automated response telling the sender that the mailbox address has changed and to contact the new email address. Our emails will be delivered from the amexgbt.com domain. For example support@hrgworldwide.com becomes usersupport.gc@amexgbt.com.

There are no changes to the current services or product functionalities.

For more information, refer to the American Express global business travel announcement (PDF, 333 KB).

Online Booking Tool 2.0

AmexGBT will upgrade the Online Booking Tool (OBT) user interface to be more intuitive and user-friendly. We will begin a gradual rollout in late 2021. All Government of Canada departments must migrate by the first quarter of 2022. Specific dates for the migration of each department will be communicated as we approach the implementation phase.

Expense Management Tool Update

On March 7, 2021, AmexGBT updated the Expense Management Tool (EMT) to add the following enhancements and changes:

  • addition of time zone indicators to printed Travel Requests and Expense Reports
  • discontinuation of fax support for capturing receipt images
